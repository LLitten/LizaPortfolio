About Me

I'm Liza Litten, a 22-year-old producer who sometimes does art from West Virginia. I first decided I wanted to get into video games from watching mid 2010s gaming YouTubers talking about the connections they made through video games. My love for organization and management was discovered when no one else on my team wanted to manage a spreadsheet, which I found I loved.
My favorite game is Xenoblade Chronicles 2 but I have the most hours in Teamfight Tactics. I love to bake and I've worked in a bakery decorating cupcakes and cakes.
